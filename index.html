What are Legos?

LEGOs are plastic building-block toys that rose to massive popularity in the mid-20th century. The word LEGO is from the Danish phrase leg godt (“play well”). The basic LEGO brick has remained the same since the 1950s, and this simple, child-friendly design has been credited with the toys’ longevity.

What are they?

LEGOs are plastic building-block toys that rose to massive popularity in the mid-20th century. The word LEGO is from the Danish phrase leg godt (“play well”). The basic LEGO brick has remained the same since the 1950s, and this simple, child-friendly design has been credited with the toys' longevity.


LEGO's Mission...

Our mission is to inspire and develop the builders of tomorrow.


The LEGO Company

The LEGO Group is a privately held company based in Billund, Denmark. The company is still owned by the Kirk Kristiansen family who founded it in 1932.
